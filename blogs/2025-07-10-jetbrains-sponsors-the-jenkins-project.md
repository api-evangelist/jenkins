We’re excited to welcome JetBrains as a new sponsor of the Jenkins project! JetBrains has generously provided free annual All Products Pack subscriptions to Jenkins core maintainers and active contributors. This subscription grants access to the full suite of JetBrains IDEs, including IntelliJ IDEA Ultimate Edition, which is widely used by Jenkins developers for writing, debugging, and maintaining the project’s codebase.
