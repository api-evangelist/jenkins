<div class="paragraph">
<p>We are thrilled to announce that the Jenkins project has been allotted 5 slots in Google Summer of Code (GSoC) 2026 program!
This is an incredible opportunity for aspiring developers to contribute to one of the most widely used open-source automation servers in the world.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>The 5 selected projects for GSoC 2026 are:</p>
</div>
<div class="ulist">
<ul>
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.jenkins.io/projects/gsoc/2026/projects/ai-chatbot-to-guide-user-workflow">AI Chatbot to Guide User Workflow</a> with <a href="https://github.com/thatsdc">Daniele Caldarigi</a> as the GSoC contributor - Helping users navigate Jenkins with smart, automated assistance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.jenkins.io/projects/gsoc/2026/projects/continue-ai-powered-chatbot-for-quick-access-to-jenkins-resources">Continue AI-Powered Chatbot for Quick Access to Jenkins Resources</a> with <a href="https://github.com/arjun-713">Mallikarjun G D</a> as the GSoC contributor - Ongoing modernization of the Jenkins documentation retrieval experience.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.jenkins.io/projects/gsoc/2026/projects/jenkins-email-notifications-using-outlook-smtp-with-oath">Jenkins email notifications using Outlook SMTP with OAuth</a> with <a href="https://github.com/Faheem12005">Mohammed Faheem</a> as the GSoC contributor - Enhancing Jenkins email notifications with secure Outlook SMTP integration.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.jenkins.io/projects/gsoc/2026/projects/plugin-modernizer-stats-visualization">Plugin Modernizer Stats Visualization</a> with <a href="https://github.com/pratikmane0112">Pratik Mane</a> as the GSoC contributor - Visualizing plugin modernization efforts with insightful statistics and trends.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.jenkins.io/projects/gsoc/2026/projects/retool-jenkins-io-website-success-stories">Retool jenkins.io website Success Stories</a> with <a href="https://github.com/Vatsal-Verma">Vatsal Verma</a> as the GSoC contributor - Showcasing inspiring success stories on the jenkins.io website to motivate and guide users in their Jenkins journey.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>These projects were selected from a pool of exciting proposals that aim to enhance the Jenkins ecosystem and improve the user experience. Each project will be mentored by experienced members of the Jenkins community, providing guidance and support throughout the summer.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>We would like to extend our heartfelt congratulations to the selected contributors and express our gratitude to all the candidates who took the time to submit their proposals. Your enthusiasm and dedication to open source are what make the Jenkins community so vibrant and innovative.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>We encourage everyone to follow the progress of these projects and support the contributors as they embark on their GSoC journey. Stay tuned for updates and insights from the contributors as they work on their projects over the summer!</p>
</div></div>
